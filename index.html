About us

Who are we?

The dynamic South African-owned software development company Matrix Systems Solutions, sometimes known as (MSS), was established in 2023 on the tenet of offering answers to corporate issues.

The business was founded on the principles of software development, which we do because we are passionate about it and because our employees enjoy working for us.

Our area of expertise at MSS is application development, including full end-to-end web and/or mobile application development, which starts with the analysis of business processes and ends with the design of software that will align business requirements with IT strategy to maximize the return on investment for the business.
